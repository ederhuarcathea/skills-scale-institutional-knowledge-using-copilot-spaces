# OctoAcme — Project Initiation Guide

## Purpose
Define the initial steps to validate and authorize work, align stakeholders, and create a lightweight plan.

## When to use
Whenever a new project idea or feature proposal is ready to be explored.

## Goals
- Confirm business need and measurable outcome
- Identify stakeholders & champions
- Define success criteria and initial timeline
- Decide go/no-go for planning

## Minimum Deliverables
- Project One-pager (Problem, Goal, Success Metrics) — created by PM with input from Business Analyst
- Stakeholder list & communication plan
- High-level timeline and key milestones
- Initial risk list
- Resource needs (team roles, rough effort estimate)
- Initial user research findings (if UX Designer is involved early)

## Initiation Checklist
- [ ] One-pager completed and reviewed by Product Lead
- [ ] Business Analyst validates business requirements and ROI potential
- [ ] UX Designer conducts preliminary user research (if user-facing feature)
- [ ] Sponsor / Stakeholder alignment (email or meeting)
- [ ] Decision: Approve to move into planning?
- [ ] Create repo or project board skeleton
- [ ] Add initial artifacts to repo (docs/ or .copilot/)

## Decision Gate
Move to planning when:
- Success metrics are clear
- Stakeholders agree on priority
- Team availability is confirmed

## Role Collaboration Example During Initiation

**Scenario**: Initiating a new customer portal feature

**Step 1 — Problem Identification**:
- Product Manager identifies customer need from support tickets and feedback
- Business Analyst conducts stakeholder interviews to quantify business impact
- Result: Problem statement with measurable pain points

**Step 2 — Initial Research**:
- UX Designer conducts user interviews with 5-8 customers
- Business Analyst analyzes competitive offerings and market trends
- Result: User needs validation and competitive insights

**Step 3 — Project One-pager Creation**:
- Project Manager drafts one-pager incorporating inputs from PM, BA, and UX
- Includes: problem statement, goals, success metrics, rough timeline
- Business Analyst validates ROI projections
- Result: Complete one-pager ready for stakeholder review

**Step 4 — Stakeholder Alignment**:
- Project Manager presents one-pager to sponsor and key stakeholders
- UX Designer shares user research highlights
- Business Analyst presents business case
- Result: Go/no-go decision and approval to proceed to planning
